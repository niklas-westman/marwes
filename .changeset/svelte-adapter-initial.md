New public Svelte adapter package with 112 component exports across 24 component families.

Includes full parity with React and Vue adapters: accordion, avatar, badge, button, card, checkbox, date-picker, dialog, divider, icon, input (incl. InputOtp, RichText, RichTextField, purpose fields), paragraph, radio, select, skeleton, slider, spacing, spinner, stat-tile, switch, tab, textarea, toast, tooltip, and typography (H1/H2/H3).

Also ships MarwesProvider, useTheme, useThemeMode, useToast, and SSR helpers.
